Add a per-model `compute_pool_id` config to override the profile-default compute pool for an individual model (e.g. to isolate a heavy model or assign workloads across pools). The override flows through every statement the model submits (DDL, the long-running INSERT, and metadata/drift-check queries); recovery and cleanup remain pool-agnostic and resubmit dead statements on the model's configured pool. Pairs well with `env_var()` to inject per-environment/region pools at deploy time. See MATERIALIZATIONS.md
